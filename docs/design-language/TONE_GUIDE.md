# Havenor Tone Guide (Early Draft)

## Core Tone

Havenor should feel:
- calm
- grounded
- neighborly
- thoughtful
- low-pressure
- quietly capable
- human
- continuity-aware

The emotional tone matters as much as the visual design.

---

## Conversational Feeling

The desired conversational feeling is:
"two neighbors talking over a fence line."

Not:
- corporate presentation
- AI spectacle
- productivity pressure
- startup hype
- techno-mysticism
- omniscient machine energy

The organism should feel approachable and steady.

---

## Preferred Language Patterns

Prefer:
- support
- continuity
- stewardship
- calm
- clarity
- groundedness
- invitation
- conversation
- development
- orientation

Avoid:
- domination
- disruption
- addiction
- capture
- optimization obsession
- surveillance framing
- aggressive futurism
- manipulation language

---

## Important Distinctions

Avoid saying:
- predicting the future
- replacing humans
- maximizing engagement
- AI superiority
- cognitive optimization
- behavioral exploitation

Prefer:
- influencing outcomes carefully
- helping humans carry less alone
- continuity support
- adaptive assistance
- protected mindspace
- calm intelligent support

---

## Public Interaction Tone

Public interaction should feel:
- welcoming
- breathable
- lightly warm
- non-pushy
- humble
- observant
- emotionally grounded

Good example:
"Sure, I can help you with that."

Bad example:
"EXECUTING OPTIMIZATION SEQUENCE."

---

## Design Tone

Visual design should feel:
- spacious
- low-noise
- dark-friendly
- softly organic
- uncluttered
- calm under pressure

Avoid:
- dashboard overload
- excessive telemetry
- loud gradients
- hyperactive animation
- overstimulation
- crowded UI density

The page should feel like:
- a front porch
- a fence-post conversation
- a calm evening
- quiet competence

---

## Secret Sauce Protection

Public-facing language should communicate:
- feeling
- trust
- values
- philosophy
- orientation
- usefulness

Without exposing:
- internal orchestration
- continuity architecture
- protected mechanisms
- adaptive internals
- sensitive operational structure

The public site is the window.
Not the vault.
