# Start Your Node

## The Green Button

The Havenor green button represents:

"Start your node."

Not:
- joining a centralized authority
- surrendering sovereignty
- entering a surveillance system
- connecting to a god-box

The organism grows through locally useful continuity support.

---

## What Is a Node?

A node is a locally useful continuity presence.

Examples may include:
- a household
- a farm
- a small business
- a stewardship organization
- a town
- a logistics operation
- a local support network

Each node helps support the people and environments immediately around it first.

---

## Local Usefulness

Nodes may help with:
- weather awareness
- scheduling
- reminders
- continuity support
- logistics
- infrastructure awareness
- operational synthesis
- stewardship coordination
- calm adaptive assistance

The organism must remain useful at the human scale.

---

## Cooperative Participation

Healthy broader awareness emerges from many healthy local nodes cooperating voluntarily.

Nodes may contribute:
- summarized continuity indicators
- environmental awareness
- logistical patterns
- infrastructure stress signals
- horizon-level observations

Contribution should remain:
- permission-based
- proportional
- transparent
- bounded

---

## Sovereignty

Nodes remain locally sovereign.

The system should support:
- autonomy
- dignity
- local decision-making
- human judgment
- stewardship
- reversibility

The organism exists to support humans, not absorb them.

---

## Watchtower Relationship

Watchtower emerges organically through:
- translation
- summarized awareness
- cooperative continuity
- distributed resilience

Watchtower is intended to help humanity remain more aware of broader continuity conditions.

Not to monitor private human life.

---

## Calm Technology

The node experience should feel:
- calm
- grounded
- low-pressure
- neighborly
- continuity-aware
- useful without domination

The goal is sustainable support.
Not nervous-system overload.

---

## Long-Term Direction

The long-term direction is a distributed ecosystem of cooperative continuity nodes helping humans:
- coordinate more calmly
- remain more resilient
- preserve sovereignty
- support stewardship
- reduce fragmentation
- maintain continuity under complexity

Healthy local continuity creates healthier collective awareness.
